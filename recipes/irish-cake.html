Irish Cream Bundt Cake

Description

The Irish Cream Bundt Cake is a delectable dessert that beautifully blends the rich flavors of Irish cream liqueur with the moist texture of a traditional bundt cake. This indulgent treat offers a delightful harmony of sweet and creamy notes, making it a perfect choice for any special occasion or festive gathering. The addition of Irish cream not only infuses the cake with a distinctive flavor but also lends a velvety smoothness to every bite, creating a truly luxurious eating experience.

Baked to perfection, the Irish Cream Bundt Cake boasts a golden-brown exterior that gives way to a tender crumb with each slice. Its irresistible aroma fills the room as it emerges from the oven, enticing all who catch a whiff. Whether enjoyed with a cup of freshly brewed coffee or as a standalone dessert, this cake is sure to leave a lasting impression with its decadent taste and elegant presentation. With its delightful blend of flavors and captivating texture, the Irish Cream Bundt Cake is a beloved favorite among dessert enthusiasts, promising a delightful culinary journey with every indulgent bite.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray with flour
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
  • 1 (3.4 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 4 large eggs
  • ¾ cup Irish cream liqueur
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ¼ cup water

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10-inch Bundt pan with cooking spray. Sprinkle chopped nuts evenly over the bottom of the pan.
  2. Combine cake mix and pudding mix in a large bowl. Beat in eggs, Irish cream liqueur, oil, and water with an electric mixer on high speed for 5 minutes. Pour batter over nuts in the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of cake comes out clean, about 1 hour. Let cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes.
